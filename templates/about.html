Overview

Project Overview

In an era where AI is capable of generating hyper-realistic images, distinguishing between real and synthetic content has become a critical challenge. This project presents a deep learning-based approach for real-time classification of AI-generated images versus real photographs.

The system leverages EfficientNetB3 and a Custom CNN trained on the CIFAKE dataset (120,000 images) and integrates Explainable AI — specifically Grad-CAM — to visualise which image regions drove the model's decision.

A Flask web application enables users to upload any image and receive instant classification with confidence scores and heatmap overlays.
